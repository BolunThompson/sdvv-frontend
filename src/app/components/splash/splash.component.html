The Voters’ Voice Initiatives are aimed at providing “we the people” a greater voice in our government. Transparency in campaign finance is critical. Providing full disclosure on the flow of money in local politics empowers citizens to hold their government accountable for decisions made. Transparency reduces the potential for corruption and increases trust between voters and their government.Learn More

The San Diego Campaign Finance Dashboard is being developed by volunteers in a non-partisan coalition as a service to our community. Data has been gathered from public records posted by the City of San Diego Clerk’s Office and the County of San Diego Registrar of Voters Public Portal in order to create the graphs and summaries for each candidate’s campaign.
